FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common inquiries about D3VIEW's CAE simulation platform, who we serve, and how engagements typically begin.

Who does D3VIEW serve?

D3VIEW supports engineering teams, CAE analysts, and research organizations across automotive, aerospace, defense, and industrial equipment sectors. Our platform scales from individual experts to global enterprise deployments, including simulation data management for finite element analysis software for aerospace and automotive crashworthiness workflows.

How do engagements typically begin?

Most partnerships start with a focused discovery call where we map your current simulation toolchain, data bottlenecks, and reporting requirements. From there, we configure a tailored pilot environment so your team can evaluate D3VIEW's engineering data management solutions with real-world CAE datasets before committing to a full rollout.

What simulation data challenges can we discuss?

You can bring any CAE-related data challenge: automated post-processing, multi-solver result comparison, correlation studies, lightweight visualization for non-experts, or building a centralized simulation data lake. Our team has deep experience with LS-DYNA, Abaqus, Nastran, ANSYS, and Beta CAE Systems SA interoperability.

Do you support enterprise teams and research labs?

Yes. We offer dedicated enterprise licensing with SSO, role-based access controls, and on-premise deployment options for classified or ITAR-restricted environments. Academic and government research labs also qualify for specialized programs that include collaborative tool development and custom data collection pipelines.

Can D3VIEW integrate with our existing CAE software?

D3VIEW is designed as an open platform. Our connectors ingest results from major solvers and pre-processors, and our REST API allows bi-directional data flow with PLM, SDM, and custom in-house tools. We routinely work with Beta CAE Systems SA environments and proprietary simulation frameworks.

Is D3VIEW suitable for smaller engineering consultancies?

Absolutely. We offer flexible team-based subscriptions that let smaller firms access the same analytics and automation capabilities as large OEMs. Many consultancies use D3VIEW to differentiate their service offerings with faster turnaround and richer CAE simulation software for automotive engineers reporting.
